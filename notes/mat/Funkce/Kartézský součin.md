# Kartézský součin
$$A = \{\circ;x;\triangle\}$$
$$B = \{a;b;c;d\}$$
$$A \times B = \{\forall[x;y]: x \in A, y \in B\}$$
$$A \times B = \{[\circ; a]; [\circ; b]; [\circ; c]; [\circ; d]; [x; a]; [x; b]; [x; c]; [x; d]; [\triangle; a]; [\triangle; b]; [\triangle; c]; [\triangle; d]\}$$

## Binární relace
*A do B*
**Libovolná podmnožina $A \times B$.**

### například:
$$R_{AB} = \{[\circ; b]; [x; c]; [\triangle; a] \}$$
